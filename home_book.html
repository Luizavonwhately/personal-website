First Universe

What does home mean to you? For me, it was an evolving concept that trhought my life showed me how no matter the age or the place, people are the real state of belonging.

In this publication, three naratives are told simuntaneously, representing the different phases of life, starting form a child perspective, changing to a teenager and landing on a more mature and dense understanding of an adult.

by using distintive typography and text treatment, the reader is able to recognize the different naratives being told.
